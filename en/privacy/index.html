Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

The site al-solutions.co.il is operated by AL Solutions (Asaf Lavi). We believe in simplicity here too: the site collects as little as possible, and what is collected is used only to get back to you.

What is collected

  • Contact form: name, email, phone (if you chose to fill it) and your message. The details reach us by email through FormSubmit, a third-party form relay service.
  • WhatsApp or email contact: whatever details you choose to send, subject to the terms of those channels.
  • Hosting: the site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like any web server, GitHub may record standard access data (such as IP address) in its server logs, per its own privacy policy.

What the site does not do

  • No cookies
  • No analytics or tracking tools
  • No selling or sharing of details with third parties for marketing
  • No marketing emails without explicit consent

What your details are used for

Only to respond to your inquiry and manage our communication with you. Correspondence is kept as part of ordinary business records.

Access, correction and deletion

Want to know what details of yours are stored, correct them or delete them? Write to asaflavi@al-solutions.co.il and we will take care of it promptly.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published on this page with a new update date.

Contact

For any privacy question: asaflavi@al-solutions.co.il
