Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 21, 2026

We consider equal access for all visitors a priority, and work to make this site accessible in accordance with the Israeli Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities regulations (Service Accessibility Adjustments, 2013) and Israeli Standard 5568, which is based on the WCAG 2.1 guidelines at level AA.

Accessibility features on this site

  • Semantic page structure with a proper heading hierarchy and labeled navigation regions
  • Full keyboard navigation, including a skip link and clear focus indicators
  • Color contrast ratios meeting at least level AA for every text and background pairing
  • Alternative text for images and descriptions for graphic elements
  • Forms with visible labels and clear guidance
  • Touch targets of at least 44 pixels
  • Support for text resizing and responsive display at any screen size
  • Respect for the user's reduced-motion preference (prefers-reduced-motion)
  • Bilingual site, Hebrew and English, with correct directionality in each language

Known limitations

The downloadable PDF files on this site may not be fully accessible to screen readers. If you have difficulty reading them, contact us and we will gladly provide the content in an alternative format.
