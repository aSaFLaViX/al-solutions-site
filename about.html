About AL Solutions

AL Solutions is an AI consultancy founded by Asaf Lavi. We specialise in building operational AI systems that harness your data, integrate seamlessly with your tech stack and deliver immediate business value.

Our Mission

To empower organisations to make smarter decisions and streamline operations using AI that is practical, secure and tailored to their unique context.

Our Approach

We combine architecture, software engineering and machine learning to deliver end‑to‑end solutions. We operate within your cloud, respect your security model and ensure that every system is maintainable by your team.

  • Expertise: Deep experience across AI, data pipelines, cloud infrastructure and UI development.
  • End‑to‑end delivery: From design and modelling to infrastructure and UX, we cover the full stack.
  • Security‑first: Solutions are deployed inside your VPC. Data never leaves your environment and permissions are respected at every layer.
  • Rapid pilots: Start small, prove impact quickly and scale from there.
